Blueberry Pie

Description

Blueberry pie is a summer staple. Pro tip from one who knows, serve Ã  la mode.

Ingredients

  • Pie dough
  • Blueberries
  • Sugar
  • Lemon juice
  • Beaten egg (for egg wash)
  • Fancy sugar for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to an ungodly temperature when it's already hot af.
  2. Roll out the pie dough into a pie pan.
  3. Add sugar and lemon juice to blueberries and let sit.
  4. Blind bake bottom crust for 15 minutes.
  5. Add blueberries and juices to blindbaked crust.
  6. And top crust, making it as fancy you'd care to make it.
  7. Brush on egg wash to the top crust.
  8. Sprinkle fancy sugar on top crust.
  9. Finish baking and eat.
