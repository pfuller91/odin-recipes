Grilled Cheese

Photograph of a grilled cheese sandwich by GrammarFascist, CC BY-SA 4.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Grilled cheese is a classic comfort food. Can you imagine anything more delicious?

Ingredients

  • Good crusty bread
  • Good melty cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a pan.
  2. Add a slice of bread into melted butter.
  3. Place cheese on bread.
  4. Add final slice of bread.
  5. Flip when bottom slice is nice and golden.
  6. Remove from heat when both slices are toasty and the cheese is melted.
  7. Cut diagonally and eat with an optional bowl of tomato soup.
